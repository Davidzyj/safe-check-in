Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Safe Check-In is designed as an offline-first family safety check-in app.

Data We Store

The app may store your name, family email address, reminder time, optional message, and check-in history on your device.

Network and Email

The app does not use an app-owned backend and does not directly send your data to our servers. When you choose to send a check-in email, iOS presents the system mail interface and you decide whether to send it.

Third Parties

Your email provider may process messages you send through the system mail app. Please review your email provider's privacy practices for details.
